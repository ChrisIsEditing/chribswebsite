SERVER TERRITORY RULES

Overview

Territories are Player-Owned areas, which each have their own designated Rules. In order to maintain peace, please follow these rules at all times! However, should you violate them, your time alive may be limited.


Africa

Just South of the Transport Hub, this masive Africa-shaped island serves as the Trade Capital.

Territory Rules:

1. No PvP.

2. No killing Villagers.

3. No Destruction.

4. Houses belong to villagers.

Tower

A massive factory-style base, currently being built into a Skyscraper. NorthEast of the Trade Hub or via the Chribs Line.

Territory Rules:

1. No Tresspassing.

2. No Entry when Entrances are closed.

3. No Theft.

4. No Destruction.

The Arena

COMING SOON! A Large Destruction-Resistant PvP Arena.

Territory Rules:

1. All PvP is fair game inside Arena.

2. No Pvp within 6 Chunks (100 Blocks) outside Arena.

3. Items Lost are not Returnable!

4. No Revenge-Seeking.

Mines

All mines outside Territories are Public Spaces.

Territory Rules:

1. Fill in dangerous Holes or Gaps

2. Do not Fight over Resources.

3. Place Torches in explored areas.

4. Share supplies with other Mining players if they need any.

Outside Territories

General Rules:

1. Any place 10 Chunks outside a Marked Territory is Fair Game.

2. No Building/Desctruction within 10 Chunks of a Territory.

3. No Cheating/Xrays/Exploits or Advantage Mods.

4. Players may set any Rule they wish for their Teritory, and all players within must follow them.
